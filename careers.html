Sales Associate

Responsibilities include but not limited to are:

Greeting customers, responding to questions, improving engagement with merchandise and providing outstanding customer service.

Directing customers to merchandise within the store, and superior product knowledge.

At least 6 months of retail experience is preferred, but not required.

Digital Marketing Manager

Responsibilities include:

Identify the latest trends and technologies affecting our industry.

Design and oversee all aspects of our digital marketing department including our marketing database, email, and display advertising campaigns.

Plan and manage our social media platforms.

Oversee and manage all contests, giveaways, and other digital projects.

Skills and requirements:

Bachelor's degree in marketing, or similar field.

Minimum of 4 years of experience in digital marketing, advertising, or similar.

Advanced knowledge on multiple social media platforms and website analytics.

Impeccable communication and interpersonal skills.

Knowledgable on recent trends and technologies, particularly in the eyewear industry.

Accounting Analyst

Responsibilities include:

Manage the accounting for fixed assets, including but not limited to creating fixed assets in the sub ledger, calculating and applying depreciation, ensuring accuracy of the schedule and reconciling the fixed asset in progress account.

Meticulously review account reconciliations submitted by various departments to ensure accuracy and correcting all detected errors or omissions.

Diligently prepare various balance sheet account reconciliations, obtain the necessary detailed transactions for the account and investigate and correct reconciling items.

Skills and requirements:

Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance, or business.

CPA certification.

Minimum of 2 years of experience in accounting or finance industry.

Proficient knowledge in MS Excel and other MS Office softwares relevant to your work.
